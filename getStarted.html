Introduction to MarketInfoNavigator:

MarketInfoNavigator is a curated collection of 153,335 images of cannabis products, gathered from Leafly across 20 U.S. states where recreational marijuana dispensaries are legalized.

Explanation of features:
- Product Name: The name of the product shown in the image
- Brand: The brand of the product shown in the image
- Category: One of 8 categories this product belongs to
- Label: Sub-level information under the Category, provided by the dispensary
- THC: Delta-9-tetrahydrocannabinol value
- CBD: Cannabidiol value
- State: The state where the dispensary is located, from which the product image was collected
- Description: Detailed description of the product, provided by the dispensary we scraped from
